During the Vietnam War the great pacifist A.J.Muste stood in front of the White House night after night with a candle.
Asked “Do you really think you'll change the country this way?”
Muste answered “I don't do this to change the country. I do this so the country won't change me.”

This is my purpose in creating this web site.
I remain committed to the UK being part of the EU.
